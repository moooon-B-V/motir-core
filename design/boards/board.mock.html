The board never loads every card. Each column shows its total count in the header (the denominator) and auto-pages the next slice purely on scroll-to-bottom (an IntersectionObserver sentinel fetches GET …/columns/[id]/cards?cursor= and appends a page) — there is no "Load more" button and no "{n} loaded" note (3.2.8). A small spinner shows while a page is in flight; a failed page leaves a minimal focusable inline retry. Tall columns virtualize (only viewport rows render — bounded DOM); every column renders at the same height regardless of card count.
