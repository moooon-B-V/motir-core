Over-limit is n > limit strictly — 5/5 is at limit and NOT warned. The warning is a peach tint + an alert icon + the n/limit label (not colour-alone, finding #35) and is announced via role="status". SOFT = advisory: an over-limit column does NOT reject drops — the 3.2.4 move contract is unchanged; dragging a card into an at/over-limit column still succeeds and the warning persists.
