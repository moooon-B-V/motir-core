5 · Motir AI — plans & subscription (a SEPARATE screen, AI only). The two billed products are independent (ADR §1), so the AI plan has its OWN screen: a user who already pays for seats should not wade through the seat plan to change AI. Reached from the Motir-AI line’s “Change plan” (panel 2) + the paywall (panel 7). Shows the CURRENT AI subscription + the ladder (Free / Standard / Pro / Max / Enterprise; Pro = recommended anchor) with the Monthly/Annual toggle. The ladder is the standard SaaS pricing pattern (Linear / Vercel / Stripe): TALL, equal-height cards in ONE row (horizontal scroll below the row’s natural width — the drawn responsive rule), each opening with a per-tier USE-CASE line (who it’s for) and a CUMULATIVE feature list (“Everything in {previous tier}, plus …”). The Motir SEAT plan lives on its own screen (panel 6) — never shown here.
