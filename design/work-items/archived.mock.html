7 · 2.9.7 — the actions cell for every canEdit × canManage combination. The two capabilities are INDEPENDENT (Restore = canEdit, Delete = canManage — the shipped assertCanManage), so a row can show Restore but not Delete, and vice-versa. Each affordance is HIDDEN, never shown-disabled, when its gate is unmet (mirroring WorkItemActionsMenu); when NEITHER is held the whole actions column is dropped (panel 4).
