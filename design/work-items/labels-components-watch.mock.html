The small-header-control grammar (--height-control · --radius-btn · --spacing-control-x). Not-watching: outline eye in --el-text-muted, count in --el-text-secondary. Watching: the eye FILLS with --el-accent (pupil knocked out in page-bg), accent border + semibold accent count, aria-pressed="true" — state carried by the accessible name ("Watch / Stop watching — N watching"), never colour alone. Click or W toggles self-watch (ignored while typing — the standard guard); the count bumps optimistically (panel 6). A viewer gets this control too — watching is not editing. The tooltip names the action + the W kbd hint.
