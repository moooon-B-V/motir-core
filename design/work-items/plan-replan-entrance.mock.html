What happens on click

Plan vs Re-plan flow — workspace handoff

✦ Plan flow

1 User clicks "Plan" on detail page or quick-view.
↓
2 Workspace opens (MOTIR-1193). Canvas shows item neighborhood. Chat: "Planning MOTIR-42…"
↓
3 AI proposes a plan. User and AI converse — refine, add, remove. Proposed nodes = dashed on canvas.
↓
4 User confirms (MOTIR-911 gate). Plan persists. Done.

✦ Re-plan flow

1 User clicks "Re-plan" on detail page or quick-view.
↓
2 Workspace opens. Chat composer pre-focused: "What's wrong? What should change?"
↓
3 User types reason as first chat turn (consumed by MOTIR-908 intent classification).
↓
4 AI responds with proposed changes. Conversation continues — refine, revise, confirm. Done work = locked.
Plan / Re-plan is a conversation, not a single message. Both flows open the shipped universal PlanningWorkspace (MOTIR-1193). Plan: no opening turn — the AI starts from the item description. Re-plan: the opening reason is the first chat turn in the workspace rail. After the opening turn, both continue as normal multi-turn conversations. From the quick-view, the modal closes — a handoff to the workspace.
