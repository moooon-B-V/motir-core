The bell mounts in TopNav's right cluster, between the ThemeToggle and the UserMenu (5.7.5 also mounts the collapsed/mobile affordance in SidebarHeader — panel 2). The count = NEW notifications since the drawer was last opened (the Jira "seen-since-open" rule, recorded in the 5.7 story module). Opening the drawer marks them seen and the badge clears — DISTINCT from per-row read state (the blue-dot in drawer.mock.html): a row can still be unread (dot showing) after the badge has cleared. Badge fill is --el-accent with --el-accent-text (white, AA in both themes) — a "you have N new" count is not an error, so NOT --el-danger red. Cap at 99+. Zero = no pill at all. Open = the bell shows its pressed --el-surface state.
