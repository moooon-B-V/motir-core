Account › Profile — name · avatar · email · password

Story 8.8 · 8.8.20 / MOTIR-1205. Flips the reserved General › Profile "Soon" placeholder into a real personal-details surface (the Linear-style Profile + Security split): edit your name, photo and sign-in email, and manage your password. Built from the shipped account-settings area shell + design-system primitives; the password flows reuse the shipped reset path, and credential vs OAuth-only users get the right password action. Five panels: resting · editing/pending/errors · change-password modal · change-email + OAuth variant + loading · dark parity.
