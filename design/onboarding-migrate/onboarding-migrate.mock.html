We couldn't finish the audit. Your index is intact, so this is safe to retry. You can also skip the audit and set your convention from clean-code defaults.
Resume — re-opening a half-finished wizard

Welcome back — pick up where you left off

Your import for acme/web is paused at Audit & convention (step 3 of 6). Connect and Index are done and saved.

Every step persists its result (MOTIR-1499's MigrateOnboarding state machine, MOTIR-931's resumable routes), so Save & exit and any drop return the user to the exact saved step — the wizard never restarts from scratch. Errors keep completed work and offer a scoped retry; a failed audit additionally offers the clean-code-defaults path (the fresh-convention route 7.14.1 already draws).
