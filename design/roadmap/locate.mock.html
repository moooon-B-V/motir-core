The Locate button joins the bottom-left cluster (right of the shipped zoom + fit-view), an --el-surface icon button (--radius-btn, --shadow-card, --height-control). Its glyph is LocateFixed (a crosshair) — deliberately UNLIKE the Maximize / Maximize2 of Expand + fit-view. Clicking it pans + zooms the canvas to the "you are here" in-progress frontier (MOTIR-1013) and lights it with the accent focus ring (the data-highlighted treatment the /-search locate already uses). Accessible name "Locate the current item".

With no frontier, Locate targets the ready nodes (the mint "Ready" highlight, MOTIR-1417).
