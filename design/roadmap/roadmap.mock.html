4

Node states — each an icon + label + tint (never colour alone, finding #35)

+ the deliberate dotted (skippable) vs. dashed (upcoming) border distinction

Done · Reviewed

A produced, reviewed tier or a completed work item. Solid border, mint “Reviewed ✓” pill, carries its captured-findings rows.

Active · frontier

Where you are now. Accent ring + “You are here” map-pin tab, aria-current="step" on the node region.

Deciding · blocking ask

Validation’s validate-demand-first ask (MOTIR-1064). Peach “Deciding” pill + a warning capture row; blocks continuing.

Skippable · DOTTED

An optional step (the two optional tiers, Design, Read your project). Dotted border + a “can skip” chip — skipped in the chat, not on the node.

Upcoming · DASHED

Not yet reached (the Plan node before planning; future epics). Dashed border, ghosted — deliberately distinct from dotted/skippable.

Drafting / Will refresh

The conductor narrates a handoff (“Drafting now…”, sparkles) or a downstream tier re-derives in a cascade (“Will refresh”, rotate). Carried from the spatial canvas.
