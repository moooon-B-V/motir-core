Epic 10 · Story 10.1 · Subtask 10.1.1 design asset — built from app/globals.css tokens. An internal operator console for Motir platform staff (moooon B.V.), gated to /admin and a 404 for everyone else. Revision #2 adds: a global search (org / workspace / project / user) in the console top bar, a segmented switcher that ties the two “Usage & cost” views together, member/seat counts at every level, and a read-only “View as tenant” inspect affordance for debugging (write impersonation stays in Story 10.3). Read-mostly. Source of truth = this HTML; see design-notes.md. Gates 10.1.4 / 10.1.5 / 10.1.6.
