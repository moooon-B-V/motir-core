Story 6.10 · Subtask 6.10.1 design asset — built from app/globals.css tokens. The Organization is the new TOP tenancy tier above the workspace (the root account, parent of N workspaces, and the billing entity). Surfaces: the org switcher in the shell, org settings, and cross-workspace member management. Mirror = Atlassian org admin / Linear workspace-Owner settings (rung 1). Billing/credit is 7.12.5 / Epic 8 and ABSENT here; the cross-org platform console is Epic 10. Source of truth = this HTML; see design-notes.md. Gates 6.10.5.

Workspace configuration

Folded in here while you have one workspace — splits into its own area when you add a second.

1 workspace
Workflows & statuses, custom fields, labels, components, automation and dashboards are workspace-scoped (they live on the Workspace row underneath — there is NO org→workspace config inheritance in the data). With one workspace they appear in this single Settings home and save to that workspace. Create a 2nd workspace and they split into a per-workspace Settings area; the new workspace is seeded by copying this one's config at creation, so it looks inherited — then both are independent and either can overwrite. These sections are owned by their own design areas and are not redrawn here.

Danger zone

Deleting the organization removes all its workspaces and data. This cannot be undone. Delete organization
