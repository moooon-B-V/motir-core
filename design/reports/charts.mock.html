6 · A11y — read as text, never colour or shape alone (finding #35)

Every chart 4.6.2 ships is conveyed as text + number, not colour/shape:

  • role="img" on the SVG with an aria-label (or aria-describedby → a <desc> sentence summarising every series + endpoint).
  • A visible text legend above each chart — the series name carried in words, with the swatch as reinforcement, never the sole signal.
  • Value labels printed on the velocity bars and at the burndown's committed baseline + end-point.
  • A data-table fallback (the <details> "View data table" disclosure) giving every series as a real <table> with row/col headers — the chart re-expressed as numbers for assistive tech and for copy-out.
  • The committed/completed bars are distinguished by text label (legend + the data table), not the blue/green pair alone — so they survive greyscale (toggle dark to spot-check) and colour-blindness.
