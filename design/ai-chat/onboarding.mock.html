Step 3 · Validate · optional

Checking your idea against the market

A look at demand and the closest competitors before you commit to building. This step is optional — skip it if you’re building for yourself or have already validated.

Validate demand before you build?
Your idea is unproven and commercial. Motir can front-load a launch slice — register a domain, ship a landing page with a “notify me” waitlist, and sequence it ahead of the build — so you collect real interest first. Waitlist signups become your green light.
Closest competitors
FreshBooks $$
Strong brand, but overbuilt for a solo freelancer.
Wave free
Free, but weak at chasing overdue payments — your edge.
Bonsai $$
Broad suite; “who’s paid me?” isn’t the focus.
Demand signals ~59M US freelancers; “late-paying clients” is a top, loudly-voiced pain. A focused “who’s paid me + auto-chase” niche is underserved between free-but-basic and full-accounting.
Risk Low build risk   Stripe + reminders is well-trodden; the wedge is focus, not novel tech.
